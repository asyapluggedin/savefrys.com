Check out Have You Visited Fry's? by Gordon Viggiano, posted in September 2020.

His story describes a brief business relationship with Fry's Electronics that didn't work out (hint: Gordon's company got merged into Amazon instead). Despite the unsuccessful business interaction, Gordon reminisces on enjoying his visits to Fry's stores with his children. He ends his story describing a more recent visit to Fry's, which shocked him just as all of us were shocked to witness the store's demise.

His final words are "Fry's, please return!"
